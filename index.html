Art within Modern Calligraphy

What it means

To me, modern calligraphy became the way I express my feelings. Writing things down in such a beauty and elegant way makes me feel like I can make time stop and relive past moments or emotions. To me, it is like a superpower.

The discovery

It all started with a gift I received from a friend. It was a calligraphy set composed of a quill pen and an inkwell. At first all I was capable of doing was stain everything around me, but with time, I practiced and learned how to handle them.

Quote about calligraphy

"Ultimately, what gets written is not words, but feeling, or what is written into the words is feeling. The hand writes the heart." - Steven Skaggs
